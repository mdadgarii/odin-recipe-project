Estamboli

Description

A nice rice dish of many names and green beans. I grew up calling it "estamboli", but another appropriate name is "lubia polo" AKA green bean rice.

Most, if not all, Persian dishes can be made vegetarian.

As this is a test website, I will be pulling from other websites for the recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 Lb (453g) Cut Green Beans
  • 1 Lb (453g) Ground Beef or Impossible Ground Beef
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 1 Small diced Onion
  • Salt, Turmeric, Ground Chili Pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Prepare your rice. See our guide to washing rice on the Homepage.
  2. Put the string beans in a pot and add boiling water to it until it covers them all, cover the lid and simmer for 10 min until the beans are tender.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pot, add the diced onions until translucent.
  4. Add the ground beef to the onion pot, stir and let fry until the beef becomes brown. Add salt, turmeric and ground chili pepper, add tomato paste, mix thoroughly.
  5. Drain the string beans and add it up to the beef pot. Mix well and leave it over low heat for few minutes (while preparing the next steps).
  6. Simmer the rice until tender, Drain the rice in a colander.
  7. In a large pot, add the rice and the beef mixture into this pot in layers. Stir the top two layers (last layer of rice and the beef mixture).
  8. Add 2 Tbsp vegetable oil, cover the pot with a large napkin and the lid.
  9. Cook for 30 minutes over low heat.
