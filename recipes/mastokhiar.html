Mast O Khiar

Description

A nice rice dish of many names and green beans. I grew up calling it "estamboli", but another appropriate name is "lubia polo" AKA green bean rice.

Most, if not all, Persian dishes can be made vegetarian.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Green Beans
  • Optional: Ground beef or Impossible Ground Beef
