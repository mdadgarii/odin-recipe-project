How to Wash Your Rice.

Just in case you're like me and hate watching videos that explains how this stuff works.

  1. Acquire Rice and Bowl.
  2. Put the rice in the bowl.
  3. Use your faucet to put water in the bowl with the rice.
  4. Use your hand to stir the rice.
  5. Drain the rice.
  6. Repeat twice or until the water runs clear.
  7. Add last bit of water. The water needs to go up to the first joint of your finger from the top of the rice.
